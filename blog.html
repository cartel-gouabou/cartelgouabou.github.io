How Computers Can Aid Skin Cancer Diagnosis: An Explainer

Welcome to a world where computers collaborate with doctors to enhance skin cancer detection. This article demystifies the science behind it, using clear visuals for easy comprehension. Based on a scientific paper, we explore the growing challenges in early melanoma detection due to the rising number of cases and a shortage of dermatologists. We delve into the role of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) in melanoma detection, likening their meticulous image analysis to that of skilled investigators. However, neural networks often appear as "black boxes," and we explain how game theory brings transparency to their decision-making process. Interested readers can dive deeper into the full article and access the open-source research code.
