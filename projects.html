DMS Logistics: Container Logistics Optimization

As a Senior Data Scientist at DMS Logistics, Arthur Cartel Foahom Gouabou is currently involved in a strategic project aimed at optimizing container logistics for our clients in the container logistics sector, including shipowners, terminals, carriers, and freight forwarders.

  • Main Objective: Improve the performance of ongoing logistics operations and anticipate future flows.
  • Responsibilities: In the context of this project, Arthur works closely with development, operations, and customer service teams to:
    • Evaluate the performance of logistics chain stakeholders.
    • Optimize appointment scheduling at terminals.
    • Automate logistics processes.
  • Methodologies: To achieve these objectives, Arthur uses a variety of tools and methodologies, including:
    • Analysis of complex datasets.
    • Creation of statistical and machine learning models to interpret and anticipate trends.
    • Integration of data from diverse sources to create streamlined data pipelines.
    • Use of statistics, modeling, and machine learning to enhance product efficiency.

DIAMELEX Project: Helping to diagnose melanoma by example

Diamelex is a research project funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR) for an amount of 513,950 euros in collaboration with the start-up Anapix medical, the Marseille Cancer Research Center (CRCM) and the Computer Science and Systems Laboratory (LIS). The project concerns the development of a computer aided diagnosis system for melanoma based on deep learning techniques. Arthur Cartel Foahom Gouabou actively participated in this project as a researcher and its research activities in this project led to the publication of five scientific papers in international and national journals and conferences.
